## Research Projects

### Chinese Investments and Socio-Environmental Impacts in Brazil
**Role:** Researcher  
**Status:** Ongoing  

This project investigates the local socio-environmental effects of Chinese investments
in the MATOPIBA region, focusing on development, environmental governance,
and community-level impacts.

---

### Brazilian Foreign Policy and Interest Groups
**Role:** Researcher  

Research agenda dedicated to the analysis of interest groups in Brazilian foreign policy,
with special emphasis on agribusiness actors and the soybean sector during recent administrations.

---

## Academic and Policy-Oriented Research

### International Food Security and Humanitarian Aid
Research on international food assistance in civil wars, examining whether humanitarian aid
acts as a relief mechanism or contributes to the intensification of conflicts.

---

### Global Governance and International Cooperation
Studies on global governance frameworks, international cooperation mechanisms,
and the role of emerging powers in reshaping international institutions.
